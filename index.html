The Curriculum

A step-by-step blueprint to professional play.

01

Foundations & Ranges

Master pre-flop charts, opening ranges, and positional awareness. Build a solid structural foundation to dictate the pace of the game.

02

Post-Flop Aggression

Learn when to c-bet, check-raise, and float. Understand texture dynamics and how to apply maximum pressure on your opponents.

03

The Art of the Bluff

Discover the mathematics of bluffing. Identify the perfect combinations, blockers, and timing to execute massive, uncallable bluffs.

04

GTO vs Exploitative

Balance your game. Know when to play mathematically perfect unexploitable poker and when to deviate to exploit weak opponents.

05

Psychology & Tells

Read the unwritten signs. Decipher body language, betting timing, and emotional leaks while cultivating an unbreakable stoic presence.

06

Bankroll Management

The secret to longevity. Learn how professionals manage variance, handle downswings, and scale their stakes safely over time.
